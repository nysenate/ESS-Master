View Travel Application Forms Generated

  1. Log in to Employee Self Service (ESS).

  2. On the top menu bar, click Travel.

  3. On the left Travel Menu, click View Travel Application.

  4. A list of travel requests you have generated appears on right.

                                      Click the image below to see additional screen and field details.

  5. On right, click the row associated with the Travel Application you want to view.

  6. The Application for Travel Approval pop-up appears.

  7. At the bottom, complete the following:

    1. To see a breakdown of your travel expenses, click Expense Summary. Click Exit when you done.

    2. To print your form, click Print. When the PDF file appears in a separate tab, click the Print icon, select your printer information, and click Print. Close the tab.

  8. To close and exit the form, click Close.
