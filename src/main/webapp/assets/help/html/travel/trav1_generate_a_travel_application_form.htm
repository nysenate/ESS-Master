Generate a Travel Application Form

  1. Log in to Employee Self Service (ESS).

  2. On the top menu bar, click Travel.

  3. On the left Travel Menu, click New Travel Application. The Purpose screen of the Travel Application appears on the right.

                                      Click the image below to see additional screen and field details.

  4. If necessary click the Travel application on behalf of drop-down arrow, and select the traveler's name.

  5. Under the Purpose of Travel section, complete the following:

    1. Click the Select your purpose of Travel drop-down arrow and select a reason.

    2. Complete the name and additional information boxes that appear underneath.  

    3. Click Next.

  6. The Outbound Segment 1 section appears.

  7. In the Outbound Segment 1 section, complete the following:

    1. Under From, make sure the location is correct.

    2. Under Travel Date, click the calendar and select your travel date.

    3. Under To, type the address of your destination.

    4. Under Mode of Travel, click the drop-down arrow and select an option.

    5. If you are traveling to another destination during this same trip, click Add Outbound Segment + and complete the fields.

    6. When done, click Next.

  8. The Return Segment 1 section appears.

  9. In the Return Segment 1 section, complete the following:

    1. Under From, make sure the location is correct.

    2. Under Travel Date, click the calendar and select your return travel date.

    3. Under To, make sure your return location is correct.

    4. Under Mode of Travel, click the drop-down arrow and select an option.

    5. If you are traveling to returning from another destination during this same trip, click Add Return Segment + and complete the fields.

    6. When done, click Next.

  10. The Miscellaneous Expenses section appears.

  11. In the Miscellaneous Expenses section, enter any expenses in the appropriate boxes.

  12. If the Meals Adjustment section appears, complete the following:

    1. Review if you qualify for a meal reimbursement.

    2. If you qualify, only uncheck the box if you DO NOT want to be reimbursed.

    3. Click Next.

  13. If the Lodging Adjustment section appears, complete the following if applicable:

    1. Review if you qualify for a lodging reimbursement.

    2. If you qualify, only uncheck the box if you DO NOT want to be reimbursed.

    3. Click Next.

  14. When your application appears, scroll down and review the information.

  15. Complete one of the following:

    • If you need to change information, click the Purpose, Outbound, Return, or Expenses button at the top and make your change.

    • If the information is correct, click the Complete Application.

  16. At the Your travel application is ready to be printed pop-up, click Go back to ESS.

  17. To print your Application for Travel Approval form:

    1. If necessary, on left, click View Travel Applications.

    2. On right, click the row associated with the Travel Application you want to print.

    3. The Application for Travel Approval pop-up appears. At the bottom, click Print.

    4. When the PDF file appears in a separate tab, click the Print icon, select your printer information, and click Print.

    5. Close the tab.

    6. Click on the previous tab and click Close.
