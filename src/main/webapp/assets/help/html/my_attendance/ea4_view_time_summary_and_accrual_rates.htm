View Time Summary and Accrual Rates

  1. Log in to Employee Self Service (ESS).

  2. On the top menu bar, click Time & Attendance.

  3. On the left Time and Attendance Menu, under My Accruals, click Accrual History. Your current personal, sick, and vacation hour accrual rates and balances appear on the right.

                                      Click the image below to see additional screen and field details.

  4. Note that the information displaying in the first row under each Avail column indicates the available personal, vacation, and sick hours that you can use as of that pay period.

  5. To view a summary of all accrual rates and balances by pay periods for a previous year, click the Filter by Year drop-down arrow and select the year.

    | NOTE | For more information regarding your accrual rates and the appropriate use of those accruals, please refer to the Overview of the Senate's Time and Attendance Plan.
