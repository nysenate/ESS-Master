Correct a Rejected Time Record

  1. Upon receiving an email from your Time and Attendance Supervisor or the Personnel Office indicating that your time record was rejected, log in to Employee Self Service (ESS).

  2. On the top menu bar, click Time & Attendance.

  3. On the left Time and Attendance Menu, under My Attendance, click Attendance Record Entry. The Attendance Record Entry appears on the right.

  4. At the top of the Attendance Record Entry, select the pay period for the time record that needs to be corrected and resubmitted.

    | NOTE | The status message "Supervisor Disapproved" appears next to the active time record that needs to be corrected.

  5. Make the appropriate corrections.

  6. Click the Submit Record button at the bottom.

  7. When "Before submitting, you must acknowledge the following..." appears, read the certification, and click I Agree.

  8. When "Your time record has been submitted" appears, click Log out of ESS to exit or click Go back to ESS to display the next time record.
