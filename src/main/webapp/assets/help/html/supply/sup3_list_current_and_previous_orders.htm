List Current and Previous Orders

  1. Log in to Employee Self Service (ESS).

  2. On the top menu bar, click Supply. The Requisition Form menu option is the default.

  3. On the right, confirm your destination code which will appear in the center or in the left corner.

    | NOTE | Your destination code is important since it indicates where the supplies that you order will be delivered.  

  4. The Requisition Form appears on the right.

  5. On the left under My Supply, click Order History.

  6. To search and display orders for a specific date range, click the From calendar icon and select the beginning date. Then click the To calendar icon and select the ending date.

    | NOTE | All orders for the specified destination code (i.e., location) will appear, not just the items you ordered.

                                      Click the image below to see additional screen and field details.

  7. In the Status box, complete any of the following:

    • To list orders that have been placed but have not been received, click Pending.

    • To list orders that are in the process of being filled and delivered to your office, click Processing.

    • To list orders that have been filled, click Completed.

    • To list all orders that have been approved to be filled, click Approved.

    • To list all orders that have been disapproved and will not be filled, click Rejected.

      | TIP | To select multiple statuses, hold down Ctrl while clicking.

  8. To view more information about an order listed, click on the order and if necessary, complete the following:

    • Click the Select Version drop-down and click an option to display status information about the various phases of the order process.

    • Click Print Page on the right to print the information.
