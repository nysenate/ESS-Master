Log In and Sign Out of ESS

Log In

  1. On your Senate computer, open a browser and display http://senateonline.nysenate.gov.

  2. On the black menu bar, click ESS.

  3. Enter your Senate userID and Senate password. Click Login.

Sign Out

  1. With ESS displayed, click the Sign Out link in the top right corner of the screen.
