About Automatic Sign Out

After 14 minutes of inactivity in Employee Self Service (ESS), the Inactive Session Timeout screen displays. Once the Timeout screen appears, you have one minute before you are automatically signed out of ESS. If the screen appears and you do not want to exit, click Continue to stay in ESS.

This Timeout screen and automatic sign out feature prevents unauthorized personnel from viewing an unattended ESS session.
